In Mithila, King Janaka announces Sita Swayamvara with a sacred challenge: lift and string Shiva Dhanush.
Great kings and princes try with pride, but none can move the divine bow.
Under Sage Vishwamitra's guidance, Rama steps forward calmly while Lakshmana watches with faith.
Rama lifts Shiva Dhanush with ease, and as he strings it, the bow breaks with a thunderous sound.
Sita garlands Rama with devotion, and Mithila celebrates their blessed union with prayers and joy.
