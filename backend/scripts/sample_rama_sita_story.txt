In the kingdom of Mithila, Princess Sita's swayamvaram begins with a sacred challenge.
Many princes gather in King Janaka's court, but none can lift Shiva's mighty bow.
Prince Rama steps forward with humility, bows to the sages, and lifts the divine bow.
As Rama strings the bow, it breaks with a thunderous sound and the hall fills with awe.
Sita joyfully garlands Rama, and everyone celebrates their union with blessings and music.
