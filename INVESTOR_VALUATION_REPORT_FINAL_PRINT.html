Valuation Method

1. Replacement Cost

A buyer is avoiding the cost and time of rebuilding a broad product surface that spans messaging, commerce, payments, analytics, admin, and cross-platform packaging.

Indicated value: $190K to $280K

2. Assembled Platform Premium

The repo shows assembled systems rather than isolated mockups: routing, monetization, reporting, admin controls, and investor-facing assets already exist in one product shell.

Indicated value: $60K to $120K

3. Readiness Discount

Missing live traction, full deployment proof, and a complete investor-grade team disclosure justify a discount versus a live operating business.

Discount: -$20K to -$40K
