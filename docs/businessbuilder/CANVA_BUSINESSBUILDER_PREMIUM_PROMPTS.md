# Canva Prompts: Premium Business Builder (HomeSphere SME Growth Studio)

Use each prompt directly in Canva Magic Design as separate screens.
Use one consistent brand kit across all screens:
- Primary `#0F766E`
- Primary Dark `#0C4F56`
- Accent Gold `#CF8F2D`
- Ink `#132132`
- Muted `#60738A`
- Border `#D7E3EE`
- Soft Surface `#EFF6FB`
- White `#FFFFFF`
- Danger `#C03939`
- Fonts: Sora (headings), Manrope (body)

---

## Prompt 1: Growth Command Center (Desktop)
Design a premium SME business-builder dashboard named "Growth Studio". Desktop 12-column grid, premium SaaS style, soft gradients, trust-first and action-first. Include KPI cards: Completion %, Pending tasks, Revenue estimate, Documents created, AI assets usage, Mini app usage limit. Include next-best-action card and quick buttons: Start Guided Launch, Generate AI Plan, Open 360 Ops.

---

## Prompt 2: Launch Wizard Flow
Create a step-by-step guided startup wizard screen for first-time entrepreneurs. Show step tracker, progress bar, active question card with large textarea, previous/next controls, and quick summary cards of all completed inputs. Style should feel beginner-friendly but enterprise-professional.

---

## Prompt 3: AI Plan + Branding Studio
Design a split-pane premium screen for AI business plan generation. Left area: plan section cards (summary, market, competitor, revenue, cost, profit, SWOT, roadmap). Right area: branding output cards with name ideas, logo directions, taglines, color swatches, poster copy, caption copy. Add primary CTA "Generate AI Plan" and secondary CTA "Generate Branding Builder".

---

## Prompt 4: Cost + Scheme Intelligence
Design a practical finance planning screen with startup cost form and result KPIs. Include one-time investment, monthly burn, monthly profit, break-even estimate. Below, add government scheme eligibility panel with profile toggles (Women, Kerala, SC/ST, Minority) and recommendation cards with fit reason + benefit tags.

---

## Prompt 5: Document + Invoice Studio
Create a premium operations screen combining document generator and invoice workflow. Include document template form, live preview, copy/download actions, generated history timeline, and invoice list with status pills and download PDF actions. Make it clean, high readability, and workflow-oriented.

---

## Prompt 6: Mini App Builder + Public Runtime Readiness
Design a creator-focused screen where a business owner can launch mini apps. Include fields for app name, slug, type, branding colors, contact details, and CTA launch button. Show mini app cards with status, public URL/slug, and "Manage in 360" action.

---

## Prompt 7: 360 Operations Center
Design a premium 360 operations screen with these blocks:
- Plan & entitlements (plan selector + limits)
- AI asset generator (poster/caption/website)
- Funnel KPI cards (views, leads, orders, paid orders, conversion rates)
- Product manager (add/remove products)
- Order lifecycle panel (confirm/complete/cancel actions)
Use dense but elegant cards with clear hierarchy.

---

## Prompt 8: Mobile Adaptation (390px)
Create a full mobile adaptation of Growth Studio at 390px width. Sticky top tabs, thumb-friendly buttons (44px min), stacked KPI cards, compact forms, collapsible sections, and strong spacing rhythm. Keep premium tone while reducing clutter.

---

## Prompt 9: Tablet Adaptation (768px)
Design tablet adaptation with two-column modular layout where possible. Preserve full functionality: wizard, AI plan, costs, schemes, documents, mini app management, and 360 operations. Ensure excellent scanability for touch.

---

## Prompt 10: Design System + Product Walkthrough Board
Create one combined board that includes:
- Color tokens with hex
- Typography scale
- Buttons (primary/secondary/ghost/danger)
- Inputs (default/focus/error)
- Cards (KPI, insight, wizard, invoice, mini app)
- Tabs (default/active/hover)
- Status pills (success/info/warning/danger)
- 1-page product walkthrough flow: Discover -> Plan -> Operate -> Convert
