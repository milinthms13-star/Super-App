NilaHub Privacy Policy

NilaHub ("we", "our", "us") operates the NilaHub mobile application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your information.

Personal Information: Name, email, phone, location (for services like rides/food), payment details (tokenized).

Usage Data: App interactions, device info, IP address, crash reports.

Third Party: Google/Apple login, backend services (Twilio, S3).

3. How We Use Information

  • Security, fraud prevention.

4. Sharing Information

With service providers, partners (delivery), legal authorities. No sale of data.
